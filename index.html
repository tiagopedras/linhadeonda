Our people.
Our team.

We are surfers, we are graduated in sports and we even have a former world bodyboard champion (twice!) in our team... But what we are really proud of is our friendship and good vibes that unites us. This is our most distinguishing feature. That’s why you are going to walk out of the ocean wearing a smile on your face!

Evolution.
Competition.

Some people will remember these experiences forever. Others become addicted... That’s why, beyond the one time events, we have our own competition teams. We taught national champions, we won the Portuguese Bodyboard Cup and our athletes are a recurrent part of the National Team in the European Championship. If your goal is evolution we have got you covered... If you want to move beyond that: Welcome to the Team!
